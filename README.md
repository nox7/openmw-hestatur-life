# Hestatur Life - Mod for OpenMW
The Lua portion of Hestatur Life.

Hestatur Life is an OpenMW-only mod that adds three new factions and over 50 new NPCs all with custom dialogue, backgrounds, and interactions to Castle Hestatur. One faction is playable and is the story behind why you now own the castle and what logistics operations you and your faction manage to turn a profit and fund the castle. Additionally, a hired band of mercenaries patrol your castle interior and exterior.

Hestatur now has a troupe of castle maids that are on a schedule and can move between cells and go through doors to tidy up the castle. You will also find a record keeper in the castle's basement where you can record your relics, and adventure log.
